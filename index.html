I'm a PhD student at the University of Washington CSE, where I work with Steve Seitz. I'm broadly interested in computer vision and graphics. My current research focus is on 3D reconstruction and realistic rendering using physics and neural representations.

I am fortunate to work with awesome collaborators including Richard Newcombe and Qi Shan, labmates at GRAIL, and mentors I met during my internship at Apple, Oculus, and Adobe. Prior to PhD, I received B.S. from Caltech, working at Pietro Perona's vision lab.
